Understanding Needs vs. Wants

Needs are essential expenses for survival (e.g., rent, groceries, utilities).
Wants are non-essential expenses for enjoyment (e.g., dining out, entertainment). We follow the 50/30/20 rule: 50% of your income for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for savings.

Examples:

  • Needs: Rent, groceries, transportation, utilities
  • Wants: Dining out, movies, subscriptions, shopping
